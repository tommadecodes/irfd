The Oscar Stool

The gentle four degree splay of the Oscar Stool legs gives the design a light yet sturdy feel whilst showcasing all of the signatory handcrafted details embodied in many of IRFD’s designs.

The Oscar stools draws on the ethos of mid-century Scandinavian designs using natural materials whilst retaining traditional craftsmanship.

Customisation

Customise the design to suit your interior by selecting the solid hardwood and choose from a range of coloured powder coated bracing bars and connectors. Timber options include American Ash and American Oak.

The Oscar Stool is finished in a traditional hand-rubbed oil.

Lead time 3 to 4 weeks.
