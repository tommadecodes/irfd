The Watson Coffee Table

The sidekick to the Holmes Table, the Watson Coffee Table showcases much of the same detailing of its bigger partner. Handcrafted from solid hardwood, the design includes a weaved tensioned nylon magazine rack to keep the upper surface free and uncluttered.

Customisation

Customise the design to suit your interior by selecting the solid hardwood and choose from a range of coloured powder coated bracing bars. Timber options include American Ash, Fijian Rimu and American Oak.

The 3mm tensioned nylon comes in black or white.

The Watson Coffee Table is finished in a traditional hand-rubbed oil.

Lead time 3 to 4 weeks.
