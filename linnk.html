The Linnk Cabinet

A high end, modern interpretation of the drinks cabinet, the Linnk Cabinet’s asymmetrical design showcases content in one half, whilst securely housing additional items in its other half.

Ergonomically dimensioned, the cabinet is suited for both residential or commercial settings. The design is handcrafted from solid hardwood, handmade leather upholstery and toughened glass paneling.

Customisation

The cabinet can be fully customised from a variety of hardwoods and range of upholstered styles to suit your interior. Custom dimensions can also be made to order.

Opening surfaces are operated with gas-pressured struts. The Linnk Kabinet highlights fine craftsmanship whilst clean lines and automatic LED illumination gives the design a light and emotive quality.
