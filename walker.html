The Walker Stool

Demonstrating the strength that can be achieved with quality joinery and craftsmanship, the Walker Stool provides a unique alternative to traditional stool design.

The form and careful profiling give the Walker Stool a visually light and refined aesthetic whilst integrating IRFD’s signature detailing.

Customisation

The design can be customised by choosing from solid American Ash or American Oak and selecting from a range of powder coated coloured braces and connectors.

The Walker Stool is finished in a traditional hand-rubbed oil.
