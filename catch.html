The Catch Desk

A stylish and well considered workspace set the precedent for the Catch Desk. Classically styled and hand crafted from solid hardwood, the design provides a generous work surface whilst a slim line soft-close and fully extendible stationary drawer allows for an orderly work environment.

The stationary drawer is partitioned in such a way that it allows for storage of A4 sized documents and a 15 Inch laptop storage zone. Signature halving joints and chamfering details are featured throughout the desk design.

Customisation

The Catch Desk can be customised by selecting from the solid hardwoods and choosing from a range of coloured powder coated drawer panels.

Timber options include American Ash, Fijian Rimu and American Oak.

Lead time 4 to 5 weeks.
