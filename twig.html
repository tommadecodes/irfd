Just Twigged Cabinet

Drawing on a previous era of drinks cabinet design, the Just Twigged Cabinet looks to offer a pared-back aesthetic through clean, uninterrupted timber grained surfaces. Subtle inclusion of solid brass hardware and interior gas-pressured struts allow the design to operate as a fully functional drinks cabinet.

The interior of the cabinet comes with a mirrored backing and interconnected LED light system with the lower level including an adjustable glass shelf for additional storage. Both the upper drop-down hatch and lower doors are operated with magnetic push mechanisms to allow for a clean timber facade.

Customisation

The Just Twigged Cabinet is hand crafted from solid hardwood and high grade matching veneer plywood and finished in a traditional hand-rubbed oil.

Available in American Ash, American Oak or Fijian Rimu. Dimensions can be customized to suit your interior.

Lead time 5 to 6 weeks.
