The Weaver Credenza

A practical and classically styled storage solution for audio and electronic equipment, the Weaver Credenza is handcrafted from solid hardwood with integrated aluminum sliders.

The perforated sliders allow for full remote transmission to equipment even when closed. All internal shelves are fully adjustable and 54mm diameter holes internally allow for easy interconnection of electronic equipment.

Customisation

Customise the credenza to suit your interior by picking the hardwood and selecting from the range of coloured powder coated sliders. Timber options include American Ash, Fijian Rimu and American Oak.

The Weaver Credenza is finished in a traditional hand-rubbed oil. Additional coloured sliders can be purchased for a cost of $141.00 per slider.

Lead time 4 to 5 weeks.
